About Me

I’ve been taking things apart to see how they work since I was young. I’ve gotten better at putting them back together over the years.

My interests range from the mechanical to the electrical to the computational. I’m currently working full time while attending the UW Full-Stack Bootcamp. I enjoy solving difficult and unusual problems and making things work perfectly.

I have experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and can wrangle Linux, Windows, and Macintosh. I also know my way around a motorcycle.
